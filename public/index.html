ERC-8004 Verify

Paste signed content to verify on-chain

Two ways to verify

S
Signature check (verify link)
Click a verify link included with signed content. The link carries the signature, hash, agent identity, and a subject line preview encoded in the URL. Verification runs automatically.
This confirms the signature was made by a registered on-chain agent. The subject line shows what was signed, but the full content is not included in the link.
or
F
Full content check (paste)
Paste the entire signed message, including the signature block at the bottom. This lets you see the full content alongside the verification result.

What gets checked
Both modes perform the same cryptographic check: the signer address is recovered from the ECDSA signature, then matched against the agent's registered wallet on Arbitrum. Everything runs in your browser. No data is sent to any server.
